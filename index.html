Who am I?

A creative person with a developers mindset.

Hi there! My name is Zymantas, I live and work in Vilnius, Lithuania. I am an artist as well as a developer. Apart from being an outdoor activities enthusiast, I also love to spend days immersed in creative projects.

My creative path started in early childhood when I loved to take apart everything to see how it works and later build something from scratch in my dad's workshop. Developing websites came later in my life when I wanted to showcase my creative work online whether it was my illustrations or products that I have crafted. It took me to build a few websites to understand that designing and developing them gives me huge pleasure.

Recently I enrolled in Web Development Bootcamp at Vilnius Coding School where I got a huge boost of knowledge. I also realized that my skills in design and technical thinking are a perfect fit for this profession.
